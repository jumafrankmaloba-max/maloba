Classic Margherita Pizza

Authentic Italian pizza with simple, fresh ingredients that highlight the beautiful flavors of tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough: Combine flour, yeast, and salt. Add water and olive oil, mix until dough forms.
  2. Knead dough on floured surface for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Place dough in oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled.
  4. Preheat oven to 500¬∞F (260¬∞C) with pizza stone if available.
  5. Divide dough into two balls. Roll out each to 12-inch circles on floured surface.
  6. Spread tomato sauce, leaving border. Top with mozzarella, garlic, and half the basil.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Top with remaining basil, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with Parmesan.
  9. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

Tips & Notes

  • For best results, use 00 flour which creates a lighter, crispier crust
  • Don't overload with toppings - Margherita is about simplicity
  • If using dry yeast, activate in warm water with sugar before adding
  • Let dough rest at room temperature for 30 minutes before shaping
  • For extra flavor, add a sprinkle of oregano to the tomato sauce
